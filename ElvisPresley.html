Elvis Presley

  • Born January 8, 1935 in Tupelo, Mississippi.
  • Active from 1953-1977. Known as the "King of Rock and Roll."
  • Singer, Guitarist, Pianist, and Actor.
  • Biggest hits include Jailhouse Rock, Can't Help Falling in Love, Suspicious Minds, and Hound Dog.
  • Best-selling solo artist in the history of recorded music, and inducted into multiple music halls of fame.
