Figureheads of Rock and Roll

Rcck and Roll is a genre of music that was born in the 1950's. It was formed out of a melting pot of other musical genres, namely Blues, Jazz, Country, and Rhytm and Blues.

Rock and Roll as a genre was not created by a single person, but by many different talented artists from multiple different genres.
Out of the countless musicians who had played a role in the creation and evolution of Rock and Roll, there are three individuals who stood out from the rest.
These individuals are generally considered to be the most influential figures in the history of Rock and Roll:
  • Little Richard
  • Chuck Berry
  • Elvis Presley
    • This list is by no means definitive, as there far too many important artists to credit. These are simply three who had an undebateabley large role in the formation of the music we know as Rock and Roll.
